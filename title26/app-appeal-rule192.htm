Rule 192. Bond to Stay Assessment and Collection

The filing of a notice of appeal does not stay assessment or collection of a deficiency redetermined by the Court unless, on or before the filing of the notice of appeal, a bond is filed with the Court in accordance with Code Section 7485. For forms of bonds, see Appendix I; for forms of power of attorney used with United States Bonds as collateral, see Appendix I.
