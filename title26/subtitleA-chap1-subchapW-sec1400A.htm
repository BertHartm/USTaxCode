§1400A. Tax-exempt economic development bonds

(a) In general

In the case of the District of Columbia Enterprise Zone, subparagraph (A) of section 1394(c)(1) (relating to limitation on amount of bonds) shall be applied by substituting “$15,000,000” for “$3,000,000”.

(b) Period of applicability

This section shall apply to bonds issued during the period beginning on January 1, 1998, and ending on December 31, 2002.

(Added Pub. L. 105–34, title VII, §701(a), Aug. 5, 1997, 111 Stat. 864.)
