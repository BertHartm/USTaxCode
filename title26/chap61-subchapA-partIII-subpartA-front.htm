Codification

Pub. L. 96–603, §1(e)(1), Dec. 28, 1980, 94 Stat. 3505, which directed that item 6034 be amended by substituting “4947(a)(2)” for “4947(a)”, could not be executed because item 6034 does not contain “4947(a)”.
